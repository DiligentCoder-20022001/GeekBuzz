GeekBuzz was found in 2019 to help the student in sharpening the skills they have. It was developerd by Siddharth.S.Chandran and Prakhar Mathur when they were doing their B.Tech in VIT Chennai. GeekBuzz consists of selected questions which help you to revise the entire concepts at a glance hence proving very essential for last minute revisions. The user also faces competition with himself as after each and every attemp the answer doesnt get displayed. He'll have to find the answer and improve his score day by day which in turn leads him to become a geek!
